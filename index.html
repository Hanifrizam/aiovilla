The Philosophy of Aio

The Resting Moon

In Maori, 'aio' means the resting phase of the moon. Built to be a restful retreat, a place for contemplation and freedom from daily pressures in the Western world.

Yin & Yang Balance

Ironically, 'ayo' in Bahasa Indonesia means 'Let's go!'. The villa sets the tone for balance: poised for as much activity as you want, and as much relaxation as you need.

The Lotus Symbolism

Like a lotus appearing serene but gaining strength from its roots in the mud, Aio represents our constant striving for balance between the divine and the mundane.

What Our Guests Say

A Little Reflection on Island Life

Life is made of contrasts, and at Villa Aio we find peace in living lightly amidst these authentic surroundings.

Community & Spirit

Selong Belanak is a thriving community. While the majority practice Islam, acceptance of other religions is a matter of course. This core of spirituality strengthens the community, producing a remarkably low crime rate and a beautiful working harmony.

Activity & Relaxation

Whether you want to surf the renowned waves, paraglide over the hills, or simply meditate to the sound of fountains. The tropical warmth and island breezes invite you to drop your cares and embrace the moment.

Nature & Luxury

Villa Aio is a celebration of beautiful natural materials. Teak wood is used extensively, roofs are covered in hand-woven bamboo skin, alongside beautiful natural stone. It is the epitome of barefoot luxury living.

Culinary & Dining

Selong Belanak is blessed with wonderful local restaurants. Alternatively, embrace the joy of staying in by cooking in our fully equipped kitchen, designed to host gatherings with a view of the sea.
